Peanut Butter & Jam Bagel

This is one of my favorite pre-workout meals. It has the right balance of carbs and fats to fuel you for your workout.

Make sure to eat this no more than 45 minutes before your weight-lifting sesh!

Ingredients:

  • Bagel
  • Regular, or reduced sugar strawberry jam
  • Organic peanut butter or cashew butter
  • Toaster

Steps

  1. Slice your bagel in half and put it into the toaster
  2. Once your bagel reaches a gold-like toasty texture, take it out
  3. Add 30ml of strawberry jam on one half of the bagel
  4. Add as much peanut butter as you like on the other half
  5. Put them together and squeeze that bad boy
  6. Enjoy!!
